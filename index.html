📋 Introduction

This Privacy Policy describes how Maha Dairy Farm ("we", "our", or "the App") collects, uses, and protects the information of users who use our mobile application (Package ID: com.mahalakshmidairy.app).

By downloading and using Maha Dairy Farm, you agree to the practices described in this Privacy Policy. If you do not agree, please do not use the App.

Maha Dairy Farm is designed for dairy farm owners, employees, and customers to manage milk delivery, cattle, attendance, billing, and farm operations.

📦 Information We Collect

1. Account Information

  • Name, mobile number, and email address (provided during registration)
  • User role: Admin, Employee, or Customer
  • Profile photo (optional)

2. Location Data

  • GPS location is used to show real-time weather and temperature at your farm
  • Location is used for delivery tracking and route mapping
  • Location is only accessed when the app is in use — we do not track location in the background

3. Camera & Device Data

  • Camera access is used for QR code and RFID scanning of cattle
  • Camera is used only when you initiate a scan — we do not access camera automatically

4. Notifications

  • Push notifications are sent for delivery updates, milk billing reminders, attendance alerts, and farm heat warnings
  • You can disable notifications at any time from your device settings

5. Farm & Business Data

  • Cattle records, milk entry data, delivery orders, customer details, employee records, and billing information you enter in the app
  • This data is stored in your farm's account and is not shared with third parties

6. Usage Data

  • Basic app usage information such as login time and feature usage to improve app performance

⚙️ How We Use Your Information

  • To provide and operate all features of the Maha Dairy Farm app
  • To process milk deliveries, billing, and customer orders
  • To display real-time weather and send heat alerts to protect cattle
  • To track delivery locations and show delivery maps
  • To manage employee attendance, salary, and task assignments
  • To generate milk bills and PDF reports
  • To send push notifications for important farm events
  • To improve app performance and fix bugs

🔒 Data Sharing & Third Parties

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information to any third party.

We use the following trusted third-party services that may process your data:

  • Google Firebase — used for authentication, real-time database, and push notifications. Firebase Privacy Policy
  • Open-Meteo — used for weather data based on your location. No personal data is sent; only GPS coordinates are used. Open-Meteo Terms
  • Google Play Services — used for app distribution and device services. Google Privacy Policy

We may disclose your information if required by law or to protect the rights and safety of our users.

🛡️ Data Storage & Security

  • All data is stored securely on Google Firebase servers
  • We use secure HTTPS connections for all data transmission
  • User passwords are never stored in plain text — authentication is handled by Firebase
  • Access to farm data is role-based — Admin, Employee, and Customer each see only their relevant information
  • We regularly review our security practices to keep your data safe

While we take all reasonable steps to protect your data, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security.

📱 App Permissions Explained

  • Location (Fine & Coarse) — Weather display, heat alerts, and delivery map tracking
  • Camera — QR code and RFID scanning for cattle identification
  • Notifications — Delivery alerts, heat warnings, billing reminders
  • Internet — Syncing data with Firebase and fetching weather
  • Storage / Files — Saving and sharing PDF milk bills
  • Vibration — Notification vibration alerts

All permissions are requested only when needed. You can manage permissions at any time from your Android device Settings → Apps → Maha Dairy Farm → Permissions.

🗂️ Data Retention

  • Your farm data (cattle, milk, billing, customers) is retained as long as your account is active
  • If you request account deletion, all your data will be permanently removed within 30 days
  • To request deletion, contact us at the email address below

👶 Children's Privacy

Maha Dairy Farm is intended for use by adults for business and farm management purposes. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under the age of 13.

If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us and we will delete it immediately.

✅ Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Correct inaccurate information in your account
  • Request deletion of your account and all associated data
  • Withdraw consent for location or notification permissions at any time
  • Request a copy of your data

To exercise any of these rights, contact us using the details below.
